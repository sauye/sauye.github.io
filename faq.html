  • Frequent Asked Questions

    • ▶ Q1. What are differences on Spring Roll of China, Thailand and Vietnam?

    "Wrapper" (Chinese & Thai use wheat flour while Vietnamese use rice flour). "Filling" (Chinese prefer veg&fruit, Thai & Vietnamese prefer seafood). "Serving" (Chinese & Thai raw roll to be deep fried, Vietnamese provide cooked filling and ready-to-use wrap).

    ▶ Q2. What is Tsingtao Spring Roll? Is it a type?

    Tsingtao Spring Roll means China-style spring roll, just a name following early stage when foreigners in China calling "spring roll".

    ▶ Q3. Can I customize the packaging?

    Yes, just send us your AI files, or we can build your design files for free.

    ▶ Q4. What is your MOQ?

    Order better to be set at least by 1x20ft. And it is fine to load different products to the same container. MOQ for every product could be 100 cartons or 1 ton. Roughly 10 tons could be loaded in 1x20ft.

    Place an Order → Prepayment → Packaging Confirmed → Producing → Booking a Shipment → Loading → Shipping → Balance Payment → Documentation → Clearing Customs.
