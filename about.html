  • About Us

    • Sauye Foods (Fujian) Co.,Ltd is a professional supplier of frozen processed foods in China.

    The founding team has been supplying frozen veg & fruit worldwide since 2000s. Over 20 years of passion for frozen foodstuffs, we are here to build a brand new named "Sauye".

    In 2019, our company invested in a new factory with two production lines which daily-output over 35 tons of IQF products, and a capacity of 9000 tons for cold storage. Being strategically located in Zhangzhou City, Fujian Province, the factory is specialized in processing IQF Finger Foods, such as Vegetable Spring Roll, Vegetable Samosa, Sesame Ball, etc. Those products are seen as essential dishes to taste the Asian Flavour, also called as "Dim sum" in Mandarin Chinese.

    Now production lines have got qualified by HACCP, BRC, ISO22000 and other Food Safety Management Systems. Our advanced facility and exceptional team will bring you a great experience.

    The Sauye brand covers a broad range of Frozen Foods, as we also have production sites to process frozen Seafoods and Vegetables.
