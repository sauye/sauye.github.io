  • Sauye Foods (Fujian) Co.,Ltd is a professional supplier of frozen processed foods in China.

    Our company invested in a new factory (2019) with two production lines which daily-output over 35 tons of IQF products, and a capacity of 9000 tons for cold storage. Being strategically located in Zhangzhou City, Fujian Province, the factory is specialized in processing (IQF) frozen Finger Foods - such as Vegetable Spring Roll, Vegetable Samosa and Sesame Ball - those proved as essential dishes to taste the Asian Flavour.

    And we also have another production sites to supply high-quality frozen Asian Seafoods and Vegetables.

    Our production lines have been qualified by HACCP, BRC, ISO22000 and other Food Safety Management Systems.
